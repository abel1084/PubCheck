# Publication Design Requirements

## Cover Page
- UNEP logo on front cover: top-right corner, minimum 20mm width, target 27.5mm, height 30mm
- UNEP logo on back cover: bottom-left corner, minimum 20mm width
- Main title: 28-34pt
- Subtitle: 12-14pt
- Cover image: minimum 300 DPI
- Partner logos: on inner pages or bottom-left of cover, smaller than UNEP logo, 10mm spacing

## Typography
- Body text: 9-12pt, Roboto Flex preferred (Roboto acceptable)
- Chapter introductions: 11-12pt
- Chapter titles (H1): Large, bold, UNEP Blue (#00AEEF)
- Section headings (H2): Bold, UNEP Blue (#00AEEF)
- Subsection headings (H3): UNEP Blue, underlined
- Sub-subsection (H4): Bold, black
- Charts: Roboto Condensed, 8pt
- Figure/table titles: 9pt bold
- Captions: 7pt Roboto

## Images
- Minimum DPI: 300 for print quality
- Maximum figure width: 160mm
- Color space: RGB or CMYK
- Chart stroke weight: 0.30-0.40pt

## Margins
- Top margin: 20-25mm
- Bottom margin: 20-30mm minimum
- Inside margin (binding): 20-30mm
- Outside margin: 20-25mm
- Full-bleed images are acceptable and intentional

## Required Elements (Critical)
- ISBN: Must be present (format: ISBN 978-xxx or 979-xxx)
- DOI: Must be present
- Job number: UNEP job number required
- Copyright notice: (C) [year] United Nations Environment Programme
- Territorial disclaimer: "designations employed...do not imply...opinion...United Nations"
- Commercial products disclaimer: "mention of...commercial products...does not imply endorsement"
- Views expressed disclaimer: "views expressed...do not necessarily reflect...United Nations"
- Suggested citation format
- SDG icons: 1-3 icons should be displayed
- Table of contents: tagged and linked
- Page numbers: Roman numerals for front matter, Arabic for main content

## Back Cover Elements
- UNEP logo
- Website URL
- Contact email
- Funding acknowledgment

## Notes
- Publications are formal UNEP documents requiring all metadata
- Pay attention to heading hierarchy consistency
- Cross-reference table of contents with actual page numbers
